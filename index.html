Welcome to White Mountain Alpine!

White Mountain Alpine is a boutique guide service catering to those looking for personalized climbing experience. Operated by New Hampshire based guide Keith Sidle, White Mountain Alpine is here to help you achieve your climbing goals and experience all the region has to offer. The White Mountains and surrounding Northeast venues are the perfect locale to push ones self and develop a well rounded climbing skill set before exploring the greater ranges of the United States and further!
